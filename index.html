Frequently Asked Questions About Braces

How much do braces typically cost at Smiles By Design Orthodontics in D'Iberville, MS?
The cost of braces at Smiles By Design Orthodontics usually ranges from $3,800 to $5,500, depending on the complexity of your case and the type of braces you choose (traditional metal, clear, or Invisalign). We offer complimentary consultations to provide you with a personalized treatment plan and a detailed cost estimate. We also accept most dental insurance plans and offer flexible in-house financing to help make treatment affordable for families in the D'Iberville and Gulf Coast area.
How long will I need to wear braces if I start treatment here?
The average treatment time for braces at our D'Iberville office is between 18 and 24 months. However, your exact timeline will depend on your unique orthodontic needs and how well you follow your treatment plan. During your initial consultation, Dr. Vo will evaluate your teeth and bite to give you a more specific estimate tailored to your situation.
Is Smiles By Design Orthodontics licensed and certified to perform orthodontic treatments?
Absolutely. Dr. Vo is a board-certified orthodontist, licensed by the Mississippi State Board of Dental Examiners, and our practice complies with all local and state regulations. Our team regularly completes continuing education to stay current on the latest orthodontic techniques and safety protocols.
What is the process for getting braces at your D'Iberville office?
The process starts with a complimentary consultation, where we'll take digital x-rays and photos to assess your teeth and bite. Dr. Vo will discuss your options and answer any questions. If you decide to move forward, we'll schedule an appointment to place your braces, which usually takes about 1-2 hours. After that, you'll visit our office every 4-8 weeks for adjustments and progress checks. Once your treatment is complete, we'll fit you with a retainer to maintain your new smile.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on orthodontic treatment?
We stand behind the quality of our work. After your braces are removed, we provide complimentary retainer checks for the first year to ensure your teeth stay in place. While orthodontic results require ongoing retainer wear, if you experience any issues with your braces or appliances during active treatment, we’ll make necessary adjustments or repairs at no additional cost.
