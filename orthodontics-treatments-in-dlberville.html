Frequently Asked Questions About Orthodontics Treatments

How much do braces or Invisalign treatments typically cost at Smiles By Design Orthodontics in D'Iberville, and are payment plans available?
At Smiles By Design Orthodontics, the cost of braces generally ranges from $4,000 to $6,000, while Invisalign treatments typically start around $4,500. We offer flexible, in-office payment plans with low or no interest, and we also accept most major dental insurances. Our team will go over all your options and help maximize any insurance benefits you may have.
How long does orthodontic treatment usually take from start to finish?
The average treatment time for braces or Invisalign at our D'Iberville office is between 18 and 24 months. Some simpler cases can be completed in as little as 12 months, while more complex corrections might take up to 30 months. During your free consultation, Dr. Tran will provide a personalized estimate based on your specific needs and explain each phase of the process.
Is Smiles By Design Orthodontics licensed and experienced, and is Dr. Tran board-certified?
Absolutely. Smiles By Design Orthodontics is fully licensed in the state of Mississippi. Dr. Tran is a board-certified orthodontist with over 15 years of experience serving families along the Gulf Coast. Our staff regularly pursues continuing education to stay up-to-date with the latest orthodontic techniques and safety standards.

Your first step is to schedule a free consultation at our D'Iberville office. During your visit, you'll receive a complete orthodontic exam, digital photos, and X-rays.
